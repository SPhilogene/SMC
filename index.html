On Friday April 25th & Saturday April 26th, hundreds gathered with us to embark on a powerful session entitled “The Reproductive Freedom Train: An Afrofuturistic Journey To Liberation”, a metaphorical journey across the United States, modeled by the historical Underground Railroad and the Negro Motorist Green Book on Zoom!

The freedom journey followed Black women, birthing people, their babies and families as they sought to “liberate” themselves from the bondage of racism, oppression, injustice and adverse outcomes in the reproductive healthcare system. The stops that they encountered on the “Freedom Train” allowed travelers to experience individuals, organizations and/or events that offer them support, education, healing, resources and inspiration to counteract the adverse impact and outcomes associated with reproductive injustice and disparities. Each stop on the journey reflected the critical elements of reproductive justice, including birth equity, trauma-informed care, advocacy, cultural competency, and anti-oppression practices.

This interactive toolkit gives you access to a comprehensive resource list of activists, providers, birthing centers, farms, and healing spaces to support their journey beyond that event.

This toolkit is a call to action, blending historical homage, cultural affirmation, and futuristic visioning to empower participants, foster healing, and imagine a world where reproductive liberation is a reality for all.
